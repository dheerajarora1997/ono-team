Our Visions

One for all, all for one/ Small steps — Big difference

We encourage communal participation via volunteerism, social drives, nukkad naatak, webinars, surveys and presentations.

NONE of us is as smart as all of US !

We aim to propagate our skills to as many as we can for a better social, emotional and future ready generation.

Aspire to be a GIVER.

Come and join our hands in this social cause. It’s time to give something back to our society. We also invite anyone who wishes to educate online about various primary and secondary level students through our online platform. EACH ONE, TEACH ONE !

India’s current Slum Population is bigger than the Entire UK.

These overpopulated urban towns with lack of hygiene and education conceal a lot of our young talents. There is a real-time need for better Govt. Policies and collaboration efforts from NGOs for the upliftment of all sections of society. Over and Out aims to provide such individuals with an interactive platform which will be easily accessible to them.
